### Package Name Updated

GalileoTify has migrated its package name from `com.galileotify.beta` to `music.galileotify.app`.

This update is required to ensure compatibility with future releases and prevent potential issues during upcoming updates.

Users are highly encouraged to update to **v15.5.0**, as older builds may experience installation conflicts, update failures, or unexpected behavior when newer versions become available.

Future releases will be distributed exclusively under the new package name.
